Basic Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with olive oil, lemon juice, garlic, oregano, smoked paprika, cumin, salt, and pepper for 20 minutes or up to 8 hours.
  2. Start rice: rinse jasmine rice, then simmer with water and salt for 15 minutes. Rest covered 10 minutes.
  3. Make quick pickled onions by combining sliced red onion, vinegar, sugar, and salt. Let sit at least 20 minutes.
  4. Make tzatziki by mixing Greek yogurt, squeezed cucumber, lemon juice, garlic, dill, olive oil, and salt.
  5. Cook chicken in a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat for 5 to 7 minutes per side, until browned and 165 degrees F inside. Rest 5 minutes, then slice.
  6. Build bowls with rice, chicken, romaine, tomato, cucumber, pickled onion, and tzatziki.

Notes

  • What might need testing: Whether rice or pita would be preferred next time.
  • Possible substitutions: Add feta if the family wants a saltier bowl.
  • Prep-ahead ideas: Make tzatziki and pickled onion Wednesday night. Chop romaine day-of.
  • Family preference concerns: Keep components separate for customizable bowls.
