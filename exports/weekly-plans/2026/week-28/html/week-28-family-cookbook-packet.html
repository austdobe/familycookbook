Prep Guide

Every prep task should include ingredients, instructions, storage method, use-by date, and meal ownership.

Sunday Dinner Support

  • Make jerk marinade.
  • Ingredients: Jerk Chicken marinade ingredients.
  • Instructions: Blend or finely mash marinade, coat chicken, and refrigerate.
  • Storage method: Covered container or zip-top bag in refrigerator.
  • Use-by date: Cook Sunday, July 5.
  • Meal ownership: Jamaican Jerk Chicken.
  • Make pineapple mango salsa.
  • Ingredients: Pineapple, mango, bell pepper, red onion, cilantro, jalapeno, lime juice, salt.
  • Instructions: Dice and mix. If making more than 2 hours ahead, hold salt until serving.
  • Storage method: Covered container in refrigerator.
  • Use-by date: Sunday, July 5.
  • Meal ownership: Jamaican Jerk Chicken.

Future Meal Prep

  • Slice Mongolian beef and broccoli.
  • Ingredients: Flank steak, broccoli.
  • Instructions: Slice beef against the grain and cut broccoli into florets.
  • Storage method: Separate covered containers in refrigerator.
  • Use-by date: Monday, July 6.
  • Meal ownership: Mongolian Beef.
  • Shape French onion meatballs.
  • Ingredients: Ground beef, egg, panko, milk, Worcestershire, garlic powder, salt, pepper.
  • Instructions: Mix gently and shape into 16 to 20 meatballs.
  • Storage method: Covered tray or container in refrigerator.
  • Use-by date: Tuesday, July 7.
  • Meal ownership: French Onion Meatballs.
  • Mix Malaysian peanut sauce and cut vegetables.
  • Ingredients: Peanut sauce ingredients, bell peppers, snow peas, carrots.
  • Instructions: Whisk sauce smooth and slice vegetables.
  • Storage method: Sauce in jar, vegetables in covered container.
  • Use-by date: Wednesday, July 8.
  • Meal ownership: Malaysian Peanut Chicken.

Leftover Plan

  • Planned leftovers: Baked ziti should produce the strongest planned leftovers. French onion meatballs may also work for lunches if the gravy holds.
  • Lunch conversions: Mongolian beef, Malaysian peanut chicken, and Greek chicken can become rice bowls.
  • Freeze portions: Baked ziti can be frozen after baking if cooled completely and wrapped well.
  • Do not save: Pineapple mango salsa may get watery after 24 hours. Romaine bowl toppings should be kept separate and used quickly.
