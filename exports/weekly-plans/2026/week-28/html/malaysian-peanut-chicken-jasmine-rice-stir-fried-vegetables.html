Basic Instructions

  1. Start rice: rinse jasmine rice, then simmer with water and salt for 15 minutes. Rest covered 10 minutes.
  2. Whisk peanut butter, coconut milk, soy sauce, fish sauce, lime juice, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, curry powder, turmeric, cumin, coriander, chili-garlic sauce, and warm water until smooth.
  3. Cook chicken in oil over medium-high heat until browned and cooked to 165 degrees F, 8 to 10 minutes.
  4. Add sauce to chicken and simmer 3 to 5 minutes, stirring often. Add splashes of water if it gets too thick.
  5. In a separate skillet or after transferring chicken out briefly, stir-fry bell peppers, snow peas, and carrots in oil with salt until crisp-tender, 4 to 6 minutes.
  6. Serve peanut chicken over rice with vegetables and cilantro.

Notes

  • What might need testing: Peanut sauce thickness and salt level. Add water gradually.
  • Possible substitutions: Use extra soy sauce instead of fish sauce if needed.
  • Prep-ahead ideas: Whisk sauce and cut vegetables the night before.
  • Family preference concerns: Keep chili-garlic sauce mild at first.
