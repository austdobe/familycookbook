# Prep Rules

Prep should make the week easier without damaging texture, freshness, or food safety.

## Required Sections

- Sunday Dinner Support
- Future Meal Prep
- Protein Thaw Schedule
- Wednesday Refresh
- Do Not Prep Ahead

## Task Requirements

Every prep task must include:

- Ingredients
- Instructions
- Storage method
- Use-by date
- Meal ownership

## Good Prep Candidates

- Wash and dry sturdy produce.
- Mix dry spice blends.
- Portion sauces that hold well.
- Thaw proteins on a clear schedule.
- Cook grains only when texture will hold for the planned meal.

## Do Not Prep Ahead Without a Reason

- Delicate herbs
- Crispy toppings
- Seafood marinades
- Cut avocado
- Anything that will make a meal worse by Wednesday
