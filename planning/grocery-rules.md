# Grocery Rules

## Non-Negotiables

- Grocery lists are recipe-driven only.
- Assume nothing is on hand.
- Do not add household staples unless required by a recipe.
- Every grocery item must list quantity, preferred version/type, acceptable alternatives, and recipe ownership.
- Use arrowroot instead of cornstarch unless the recipe specifically requires cornstarch.

## Required Item Format

| Quantity | Item | Preferred version/type | Acceptable alternatives | Recipe |
|---:|---|---|---|---|
| 2 lb | Chicken thighs | Boneless skinless | Chicken breast if adjusted for cook time | Example recipe |

## Quality Checks

- Can someone shop from the list without asking follow-up questions?
- Does every item belong to a recipe?
- Are perishables tied to the week schedule?
- Are proteins specified by cut, weight, and condition such as fresh, frozen, bone-in, or boneless?
