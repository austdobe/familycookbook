## Standing Lessons

- Protein-forward meals are more satisfying for the family.
- International variety keeps weekly plans from feeling repetitive.
- Perishability-first scheduling prevents waste and improves quality.
- Clear grocery specifications reduce shopping mistakes.
- Arrowroot is preferred over cornstarch.
