## Planning Summary

- Servings: 4
- Estimated prep time: 20 minutes
- Estimated cook time: 35 minutes
- Protein: Pumpkin seeds plus optional chicken
- Cuisine or flavor direction: Fall grain bowl, roasted vegetables, maple Dijon dressing
- Best day to cook: Any weeknight
- Perishability notes: Uses sweet potatoes, Brussels sprouts, and spinach. Best while spinach is fresh.

- Baking sheet

## Basic Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Toss sweet potatoes and Brussels sprouts with 2 tbsp olive oil, salt, and pepper.
2. Roast 25 to 35 minutes, stirring once, until browned and tender.
3. Cook brown rice or quinoa according to package directions.
4. Whisk 1 tbsp olive oil, Dijon, maple syrup, apple cider vinegar, and a pinch of salt.
5. Assemble bowls with grain, spinach, roasted vegetables, pumpkin seeds, dried cranberries, and dressing.

## Notes

- What might need testing: Whether this needs added chicken to be filling enough.
- Possible substitutions: Add grilled chicken, turkey meatballs, or salmon for more protein.
- Prep-ahead ideas: Cook grain and roast vegetables ahead; dress when serving.
- Family preference concerns: Dried cranberries make it sweeter; reduce if needed.
