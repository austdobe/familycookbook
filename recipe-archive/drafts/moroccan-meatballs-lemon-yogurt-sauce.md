## Basic Instructions

1. Mix beef, egg, breadcrumbs, 3 minced garlic cloves, cumin, paprika, coriander, cinnamon, salt, pepper, and parsley until just combined.
2. Form 20 to 24 meatballs.
3. For skillet cooking, heat oil over medium-high heat and brown meatballs in batches. Lower heat, cover, and cook until the centers reach 160 degrees F.
4. For oven cooking, bake meatballs on a sheet pan at 400 degrees F for 18 to 20 minutes, until browned and cooked through.
5. Mix yogurt, lemon juice, 1 minced garlic clove, salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
6. Serve meatballs over rice or couscous with lemon yogurt sauce.

## Notes

- What might need testing: Cinnamon level and whether the sauce needs more lemon.
- Possible substitutions: Ground lamb can make this richer; ground turkey may need 1 tbsp olive oil in the mixture.
- Prep-ahead ideas: Shape meatballs up to 24 hours ahead and refrigerate covered.
- Family preference concerns: Keep sauce on the side for anyone sensitive to tangy yogurt sauces.
