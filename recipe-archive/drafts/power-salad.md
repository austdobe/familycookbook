## Planning Summary

- Servings: 4
- Estimated prep time: 25 minutes
- Estimated cook time: 15 minutes if cooking chicken
- Protein: Grilled chicken and hard boiled eggs
- Cuisine or flavor direction: Protein-forward chopped salad, vinaigrette

## Equipment

- Mixing bowl
- Cutting board and knife
- Skillet or grill pan if cooking chicken
- Saucepan if boiling eggs

## Basic Instructions

1. Cook chicken if needed, then rest and slice. Chicken should reach 165 degrees F.
2. Hard boil eggs if needed, then cool, peel, and halve or chop.
3. Prepare cucumber, tomatoes, and bell peppers.
4. Whisk olive oil, vinegar, Dijon, honey, salt, and pepper.
5. Assemble greens, chicken, eggs, vegetables, cheese, and sunflower seeds.
6. Add dressing just before serving.

## Notes

- What might need testing: Dressing ratio and whether this is filling enough for dinner.
- Possible substitutions: Turkey, steak, or salmon can replace chicken.
- Prep-ahead ideas: Cook chicken and eggs ahead. Store dressing separately.
- Family preference concerns: Wet ingredients should stay separate until serving to prevent wilting.
