## Basic Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Toss Brussels sprouts, zucchini, squash, and mushrooms with 2 tbsp olive oil, Italian seasoning, half the salt, and half the pepper.
2. Roast vegetables 22 to 28 minutes, stirring once, until browned and tender.
3. Pat steak bites dry. Season with garlic powder, onion powder, paprika, remaining salt, and remaining pepper.
4. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a cast iron skillet over medium-high heat. Sear steak in batches so pieces brown instead of steam.
5. Add butter at the end and toss steak bites until glossy. Pull steak around 130 to 135 degrees F for medium-rare or 140 to 145 degrees F for medium.
6. Serve steak bites with roasted vegetables.

## Notes

- What might need testing: Steak doneness and vegetable mix.
- Possible substitutions: Baby potatoes can be added if a starch is needed.
- Prep-ahead ideas: Cut vegetables earlier in the day; cut steak just before cooking if possible.
- Family preference concerns: Avoid crowding the skillet or the steak will not brown well.
