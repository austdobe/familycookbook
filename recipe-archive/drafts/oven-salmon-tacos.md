# Oven Salmon Tacos

## Planning Summary

- Servings: 4
- Estimated prep time: 20 minutes
- Estimated cook time: 15 minutes
- Protein: Salmon
- Cuisine or flavor direction: Fish tacos, lemon yogurt slaw, mild and fresh
- Best day to cook: Early week
- Perishability notes: Uses salmon and slaw vegetables. Cook salmon within 1 day of shopping unless frozen.

## Equipment

- Baking sheet
- Mixing bowl
- Cutting board and knife
- Fork for flaking salmon

## Ingredients

Assume nothing is on hand. Include quantities for everything used.

| Quantity | Ingredient | Preferred version/type | Acceptable alternatives | Notes |
|---:|---|---|---|---|
| 1 1/2 lb | Salmon | Skin-on or skinless fillet | Frozen salmon, thawed | Main protein |
| 1 tbsp | Olive oil | Extra-virgin or regular | Avocado oil | Salmon |
| 1 tsp | Kosher salt | Kosher | Fine sea salt, use less | Salmon and slaw |
| 1/2 tsp | Black pepper | Ground | Freshly ground | Salmon and slaw |
| 12 | Corn tortillas | Street taco or standard size | Flour tortillas | Serving |
| 4 cups | Green cabbage | Thinly shredded | Bagged coleslaw mix | Slaw |
| 2 | Carrots | Shredded | Matchstick carrots | Slaw |
| 1/2 cup | Plain Greek yogurt | Full-fat or 2 percent | Sour cream | Slaw dressing |
| 2 tbsp | Lemon juice | Fresh | Bottled lemon juice | Slaw |
| 1 tbsp | Honey | Liquid honey | Maple syrup | Slaw |
| 1/2 tsp | Garlic powder | Ground | 1 minced garlic clove | Slaw |

## Basic Instructions

1. Heat oven to 400 degrees F. Place salmon on a baking sheet and rub with olive oil, half the salt, and half the pepper.
2. Bake salmon 12 to 15 minutes, until it flakes easily and reaches 125 to 135 degrees F depending on preferred doneness.
3. Mix Greek yogurt, lemon juice, honey, garlic powder, remaining salt, and remaining pepper.
4. Toss cabbage and carrots with yogurt dressing.
5. Warm tortillas.
6. Flake salmon and assemble tacos with slaw.

## Notes

- What might need testing: Salmon doneness and slaw sweetness.
- Possible substitutions: Cod or mahi mahi can work with adjusted cook time.
- Prep-ahead ideas: Shred vegetables earlier, but dress slaw shortly before serving.
- Family preference concerns: Keep slaw separate so tortillas do not get soggy.
