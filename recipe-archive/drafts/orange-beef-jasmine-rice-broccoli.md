## Basic Instructions

1. Start rice: rinse jasmine rice, then simmer with water and salt for 15 minutes. Rest covered 10 minutes.
2. Whisk orange juice, orange zest, soy sauce, rice vinegar, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, and red pepper flakes.
3. Toss sliced beef with arrowroot until lightly coated.
4. Stir-fry broccoli in 1 tbsp oil with salt until bright green and crisp-tender, 5 to 7 minutes. Transfer to a plate.
5. Sear beef in batches in 3 tbsp oil over medium-high heat. Let the beef brown before turning so the coating gets lightly crisp.
6. Return beef to the skillet, pour in sauce, and simmer 2 to 4 minutes, until glossy and thick enough to coat the beef.
7. Serve orange beef and broccoli over rice. Top with green onions.

## Notes

- What might need testing: Whether the sauce should be sweeter or more orange-forward.
- Possible substitutions: Chicken thighs can work, but cook to 165 degrees F.
- Prep-ahead ideas: Slice beef and broccoli Sunday night. Keep sauce ingredients measured but do not add arrowroot to sauce.
- Family preference concerns: Keep broccoli crisp-tender so the meal still feels quick and fresh.
