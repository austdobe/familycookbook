## Basic Instructions

1. Heat oven to 425 degrees F. Toss broccoli, bell peppers, and zucchini with olive oil, salt, and pepper on a sheet pan.
2. Mix turkey, egg, panko, feta, garlic, lemon zest, lemon juice, parsley, dill, oregano, salt, and pepper. Shape into 18 to 22 meatballs.
3. Place meatballs on the sheet pan with vegetables or on a second pan. Roast 18 to 22 minutes, until meatballs reach 165 degrees F and vegetables are browned.
4. Cook rice with water, olive oil, and salt. Rest covered 10 minutes, then fluff with lemon juice.
5. Make tzatziki by mixing Greek yogurt, squeezed cucumber, garlic, dill, lemon juice, and salt.
6. Serve meatballs over lemon rice with roasted vegetables and tzatziki.

## Notes

- What might need testing: Turkey meatball moisture. Add an extra spoonful of yogurt if the mixture seems dry.
- Possible substitutions: Ground chicken works, but may be softer to shape.
- Prep-ahead ideas: Make tzatziki Wednesday night. Shape meatballs Thursday morning if cooking that evening.
- Family preference concerns: Keep feta moderate so the meatballs do not turn too salty.
