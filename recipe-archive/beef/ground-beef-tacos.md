## Basic Instructions

1. Brown ground beef in a skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it into small crumbles.
2. Drain excess grease if needed.
3. Add taco seasoning and water. Simmer 3 to 5 minutes, until the beef is coated and saucy.
4. Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave.
5. Assemble tacos with beef, lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream.

## Notes

- What might need testing: Preferred seasoning level and tortilla type.
- Possible substitutions: Ground turkey works, but may need 1 tbsp oil for browning.
- Prep-ahead ideas: Chop lettuce and tomatoes earlier the same day.
- Family preference concerns: Keep toppings separate for picky assembly.
