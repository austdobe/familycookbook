## Basic Instructions

1. Lay wraps flat and spread each with honey mustard.
2. Layer turkey, apple slices, cheddar, and mixed greens.
3. Roll tightly, folding in the sides as you roll.
4. Slice in half if desired. Serve immediately or wrap tightly for lunch.

## Notes

- What might need testing: Whether apples stay crisp enough for packed lunches.
- Possible substitutions: Add bacon or use Swiss for a less sweet wrap.
- Prep-ahead ideas: Slice apples just before assembly, or toss with a little lemon juice.
- Family preference concerns: Too much honey mustard can make wraps soggy.
