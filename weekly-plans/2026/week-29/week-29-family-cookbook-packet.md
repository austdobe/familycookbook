## Prep Guide

Every prep task should include ingredients, instructions, storage method, use-by date, and meal ownership.

### Sunday Dinner Support

- [ ] Make Peruvian chicken marinade.
  - Ingredients: Garlic, lime juice, neutral oil, cumin, smoked paprika, oregano, salt, pepper.
  - Instructions: Mix marinade, coat chicken thoroughly, and refrigerate.
  - Storage method: Covered container or zip-top bag in refrigerator.
  - Use-by date: Cook Sunday, July 12.
  - Meal ownership: Peruvian Chicken.
- [ ] Make green sauce.
  - Ingredients: Cilantro, jalapeno, garlic, lime juice, Greek yogurt, mayonnaise, salt.
  - Instructions: Blend until smooth. Taste for salt and heat.
  - Storage method: Covered jar in refrigerator.
  - Use-by date: Sunday, July 12, or Monday for leftovers.
  - Meal ownership: Peruvian Chicken.

### Future Meal Prep

- [ ] Slice orange beef and cut broccoli.
  - Ingredients: Flank steak, broccoli.
  - Instructions: Slice beef thinly against the grain and cut broccoli into florets.
  - Storage method: Separate covered containers in refrigerator.
  - Use-by date: Monday, July 13.
  - Meal ownership: Orange Beef.
- [ ] Prep cordon bleu skillet components.
  - Ingredients: Chicken breast, ham, Swiss, potatoes.
  - Instructions: Cut chicken into even cutlets or chunks, slice ham and cheese, and peel potatoes if desired.
  - Storage method: Separate covered containers in refrigerator.
  - Use-by date: Tuesday, July 14.
  - Meal ownership: Chicken Cordon Bleu Skillet.
- [ ] Make Vietnamese pickled vegetables.
  - Ingredients: Carrots, cucumber, radishes, rice vinegar, sugar, salt.
  - Instructions: Slice vegetables thinly, stir with quick pickle brine, and refrigerate.
  - Storage method: Covered jar or container in refrigerator.
  - Use-by date: Wednesday, July 15.
  - Meal ownership: Lemongrass Pork Bowls.
