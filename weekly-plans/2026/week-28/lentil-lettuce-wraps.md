# Lentil Lettuce Wraps

Weekly folder: `weekly-plans/2026/week-28/`

Source or inspiration: Previous family draft recipe, added for Ally's lunch  
Date added: 2026-07-04  

## Planning Summary

- Servings: 4
- Estimated prep time: 15 minutes
- Estimated cook time: 25 minutes
- Protein: Red lentils
- Cuisine or flavor direction: Lettuce wraps, soy-ginger, savory
- Best day to cook: Lunch prep
- Perishability notes: Uses butter lettuce, onion, garlic, carrots, and ginger. Lettuce should be used while crisp.

## Equipment

- Saucepan
- Skillet
- Cutting board and knife
- Spoon or spatula

## Ingredients

Assume nothing is on hand. Include quantities for everything used.

| Quantity | Ingredient | Preferred version/type | Acceptable alternatives | Notes |
|---:|---|---|---|---|
| 1 cup | Red lentils | Dry red lentils | Brown lentils, longer cook time | Base |
| 2 cups | Broth | Low-sodium chicken or vegetable broth | Water plus salt | Lentils |
| 1 | Yellow onion | Diced | White onion | Filling |
| 3 cloves | Garlic | Fresh minced | 1 tsp garlic powder | Filling |
| 2 | Carrots | Finely diced or shredded | Matchstick carrots | Filling |
| 1 tbsp | Fresh ginger | Grated | 1 tsp ground ginger | Filling |
| 1/4 cup | Low-sodium soy sauce | Low-sodium | Liquid aminos or tamari | Sauce |
| 1 tbsp | Sesame oil | Toasted preferred | Neutral oil | Sauce |
| 1 tbsp | Neutral oil | Avocado or canola oil | Vegetable oil | Cooking |
| 1 head | Butter lettuce | Whole leaves | Romaine leaves | Serving |

## Basic Instructions

1. Simmer lentils with broth until soft and thick, 12 to 15 minutes.
2. Heat oil in a skillet. Cook onion, carrots, garlic, and ginger until vegetables soften, 4 to 6 minutes.
3. Stir in cooked lentils, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Simmer until thick enough to spoon into lettuce leaves.
4. Cool filling before packing for lunches if not serving immediately.
5. Spoon filling into butter lettuce leaves just before eating.

## Notes

- What might need testing: Whether Ally prefers lentil-only filling or wants an added meat protein in a future version.
- Possible substitutions: Ground beef or ground turkey can be added, but it is not included in this week's grocery list because it is optional.
- Prep-ahead ideas: Cook filling ahead and store separately from lettuce leaves.
- Family preference concerns: Filling needs to be thick, not soupy, or lettuce wraps will be messy.
